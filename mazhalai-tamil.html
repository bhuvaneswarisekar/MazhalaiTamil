Our Vision

Mazhalai Tamil (மழலை தமிழ்) — "mazhalai" means the sweet babbling of children — is designed to bring the beauty of the Tamil language to the youngest learners. We believe that language is the root of culture, and giving children a joyful introduction to Tamil is a gift that lasts a lifetime.

The app will use interactive pictures, and audio guided by native speakers, helping children recognise Tamil letters and words and introduce them to practice writing letters — all without feeling like schoolwork.
